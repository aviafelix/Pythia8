Version

The settings on this page should not be changed by the ordinary user, but appear here for documentation purposes, and so that they can form part od the standard databases and be queried accordingly.

parm name="Pythia:versionNumber" default="8.070"
Version and subversion number, with three significant decimals.

mode name="Pythia:versionDate" default="20070105"
Last date of change of current (sub)version, in format yyyymmdd.
